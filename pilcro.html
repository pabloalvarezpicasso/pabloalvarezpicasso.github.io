Pilcro

I worked in a team of three to redesign the onboarding flow of Pilcro, a start-up based in London that offers a digital solution to brand assets management, in order to increase downloads of the desktop app. They found that users downloading it were the most sticky ones.

The final prototype and the outcomes of the research was presented to the company after three weeks of work. The main suggestions were implemented with success by them.

Competitive Analysis



We looked at the three major competitors, founding that all three have a gather access, meaning that users have to create an account to be able to interact with the principal artboard or canvas of the tool.

Qualitative Interview and Contextual Enquiry

Asking people within the target of the company about their day to day brand styles and files management, and to start interacting with the product we found this insights.
