Nequi


I was part of the multidisciplinary team that carried out the initial understanding and ideation to disrupt the banking industry of Latin America. The project followed an agile methodology based on springs, where banking experts, design researchers, business, service and digital designers worked together along all the process.

I was specially involved in the communication tasks of the project, producing visual panels, story tellings, illustrations, infographics and more.

I collaborated in making and transcribing semi-structured Interviews and making sense of a large quantity of data gather from surveys, cart sorting, diaries of use and more design research methods. I also had an active rol in the first visual and interactive inspirational explorations, the planning and facilitation of service design workshops, the ideation of design scenarios and documentation of the process.
