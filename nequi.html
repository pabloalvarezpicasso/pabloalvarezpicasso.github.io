Nequi


The project followed an agile methodology based on Design Sprints. Banking experts, design researchers, business, service and digital designers worked together along the conceptualization phase.



We conducted a set of research methods: semi-structured interviews, focus groups, surveys and diaries of use. Then, made sense of all the gathered data, and built upon the main insights to generate different design scenarios.



I was specially involved in the graphic communication side of the project, producing visual panels, stories, infographics and more visual artefacts.
