Nequi


The project followed a methodology based on Design Sprints. Financial Experts, Design Researchers, Business, Service and Digital Designers worked together to conceptualise a banking app in Medell√≠n, Colombia.

I was specially involved in the graphic communication side of the project, producing visual panels, stories, infographics and more visual artefacts.
