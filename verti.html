Verti

I worked as part of Verti's digital team, providing support for UX design for 2 months.

1. Communicating changes in the bill.

A complex and personalized pricing in the insurance business let to a design challenge: to give clients the right information to understand why they pay a certain amount.



We went trough the user journey of annual service renovation, and worked hand by hand with IT to establish a more transparent way of communicating the price. Then we desined and implemented the solution.

2. Home Insurance Calculator Implementation

After an IT implementation that allowed the user to save time in his home insurance calculation, data science team detected a vanishing point at the first step of the process.



A/B tests were carried out changing the way and order the questions were presented. The changes made a significant positive impact in users dropping out rate.
