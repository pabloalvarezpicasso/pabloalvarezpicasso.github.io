Verti

I worked as part of Verti's digital team, providing support for UX design and development challenges for 2 months.

1. Communicating changes in the bill.

A complex and personalized pricing in the insurance industry leads to great design challenges, the question is: how to inform our clients about what are they paying for?

We went trough the user journey of annual service renovation , and worked hand by hand with the development department to establish a more transparent way of communicating the price to the client.

2. Home Insurance Calculator Implementation

After an IT implementation that allowed the user to save time in his home insurance calculation, data science team detected a vanishing point at the first step of the process.

A/B tests were carried out around the communication of the form and the order of the questions. The changes made a positive impact in users dropping out rate.
