Smart Sleep



The project took place between Warsaw (Poland) and London. Our client had renting apartment business that was about to grow from 10-15 apartments in Warsaw to around 200 in different polish cities within one year. He faced the challenge of make this huge growth happen without losing customer service quality, his main business’ value. He contacted us to create an app that helped him on this process and also gave extra value to his service.

First steps

We traveled to Poland to meet our client and his business. After a long interview with the them, we made a list of facts and challenges and then a brief ideation of ideas and features the app could have.



User research

We conducted several qualitative interviews to different apartment/hotel users. That helped us to better understand what people expect and what they like from this kind of service.

Interview quotes

"I don't want to waste time whenever I travel for work"

"I would appreciate suggestions about what to visit in the area"

"I make sure when booking a hotel in a different country that they will be able to communicate in my language"


User segmentation

Potential users of this app would be business travellers and tourist who had to spend some time in Poland.
Business people don’t use to spend much time on booking apartments or hotels, they want services as quickest as possible and don’t want to be worried or bothered by details on the booking.
Tourist may spend more time but want other commodities as information extra or customer services available 24/7.





App features and sections

We did a short list of the main features the app would have and the basic architecture.

  • Booking section
    • Trips search
    • Last trips
    • You may like
  • Travel section
    • Travel guide
    • Tips for your stay
    • You may need
    • Currency details
    • Weather details
  • Stay section
    • Home guide
    • Services offer
    • Client support
    • Area suggestions
